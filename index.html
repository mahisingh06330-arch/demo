Cutting-Edge Research Facilities

Our institute is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support advanced research in various fields of science and technology. From high-resolution microscopy to advanced fabrication labs, we provide researchers with the tools they need to innovate and excel.

🔬

Advanced Microscopy

Electron and confocal microscopes for high-resolution imaging and analysis.

📊

Spectroscopy Labs

Equipped with UV-Vis, FTIR, and NMR spectrometers for comprehensive material analysis.

🏭

Fabrication Facilities

Cleanroom environments with lithography and deposition tools for device fabrication.

💻

Computational Resources

High-performance computing clusters for simulations and complex data analysis.

🧪

Sample Preparation Labs

Facilities for chemical synthesis, sample mounting, and preparation.

🎓

Training and Support

Workshops and expert guidance to help users make the most of facility resources.
